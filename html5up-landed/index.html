SNAP A SIMPLE SELFIE TO CLOCK IN!

Have you ever wanted a simple and convenient way to track attendance for your classroom or group? Well, now there's a way. AttendToday is an online attendance tracker that helps you simplify the tedious task of keeping attendance.

While we are building AttendToday for school teachers initially, the app can also be used perfectly for tracking attendance online for any class, group, or gathering! It can be used to track Yoga class attendance, Church groups, Martial arts classes, Child care facilities, Employee attendance and at many other places.
In order to achieve a higher accuracy of the recognition algorithm, the DNN required a lot more training with larger datasets, so we excluded it from our PoC development pipeline. Our experiments resulted in the following high-performing combination: a TensorFlow model with the YOLO algorithm for face detection, no additional face alignment, and the improved Cosine Similarity for verification.
